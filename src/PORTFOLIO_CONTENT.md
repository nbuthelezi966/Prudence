## ğŸ”„ Design Process

### My Design Process

I follow a structured yet flexible design process rooted in Design Thinking, ensuring that every design decision is grounded in real user needs.

**1. Empathize**
I begin by understanding the user's world â€” observing workflows, asking the right questions, and gathering insights about their pain points and motivations.

**2. Define**
Next, I synthesize the information into a clear problem statement. This helps me stay focused on the real challenge rather than symptoms or assumptions.

**3. Ideate**
Using sketches, brainstorming, and concept exploration, I generate a range of possible solutions. Here I push beyond the obvious and explore creative alternatives.

**4. Prototype**
I convert ideas into low- and mid-fidelity wireframes, advancing toward high-fidelity UI that reflects the final direction. Each prototype is functional enough to simulate real use.

**5. Test**
I evaluate the design with users or simulated scenarios, gathering feedback to refine the experience. Iteration happens quickly and intentionally to improve clarity and performance.
